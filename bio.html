Hi, my name is Timothy, and I started Timanson Builders more than 25 years ago. At Timanson we cover all commercial and residential remodeling needs. We offer services remodeling kitchens: including flooring, electrical, plumbing, and cabinetry. We also have years of experience remodeling bathrooms: including tile, electrical, plumbing, and fixtures. Exterior remodels, such as decks and other landscaping services are also available. And I'm passionate about adapted accessibility and remodeling projects for the disabled.

Timanson Builders is a certified general contractor, a certified Green Builder (Certified Nudura Installer), has obtained an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau, and is bonded and insured. Our company primarily serves the Denver - Boulder area but can extend out around the front range. At Timanson Builders, we offer high quality with high experience, from the original design to the finished product!
